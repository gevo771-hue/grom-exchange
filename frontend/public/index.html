Frequently asked
6 articles

        
How does hybrid custody work?
Trading balances (Spot, Futures, Binary) are custodied for instant fills and risk controls. On-chain swaps and withdrawals use your connected wallet — your keys, your transaction. You choose the mode per action.

        
What are the binary options payouts?
Payouts are fixed per pair (85–94%) and shown before you place the trade. Maximum loss is capped at your stake. No margin, no liquidations.

        
How do deposits work?
Every supported asset has a unique on-chain address. Deposits credit after N confirmations (6 for BTC, 12 for ETH). USDT is accepted on ERC-20, TRC-20 and BEP-20.

        
What fees does GROM charge?
Spot: 0.10% maker / 0.10% taker. Futures: 0.02% maker / 0.05% taker. Binary: no spread — payout is net. Withdrawals: network fee only.

        
Which jurisdictions are supported?
GROM is available in 140+ countries. Restricted regions are shown at sign-up. KYC is optional for spot and binary under daily limits.

        
How do I contact a human?
Email support@grom.exchange for account, product, or execution issues. Include the pair, UTC timestamp, and screenshots when relevant so support can reproduce the issue quickly.
